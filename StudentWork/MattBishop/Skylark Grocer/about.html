Expect more.

Sylark Grocer started out as a local, neighborhood business that catered to the growing demand for organic, local produce. Since 1982 we have been serving Portland proudly - we offer only the best goods from sustainable growers and producers and, whenever possible, only purchase from Pacific Northwest providers. We believe that the choices people make with their purchases help create a more sustainable future!

Find us

We are located in beautiful downtown Portland, OR - just minutes away from all major freeways and the Interstate. We have convenient, free onsite parking while you shop. Need to get in touch with us? Do that here!
