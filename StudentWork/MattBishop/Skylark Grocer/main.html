Where food matters

We source all of our ingredients from local producers and manufacturers whenever possible. If we stock produce or goods from outside the Pacific Northwest we choose the most sustainable option available. If you have any questions or concerns about our sourcing, feel free to address the issue with one of our helpful staff members. While we strive to be the most friendly and affordable option in town, our underlying principe is food matters.

We make shopping easy

Not only do we offer the best selection of produce, dry goods, meats, cheeses, beer and wine in town, we also strive to make your shopping experience the best it can be. Feel free to come in and browse all of our great items or use our convenient, online shopping form to create a printable, custom grocery list for your next visit. Find it here.
